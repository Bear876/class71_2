Campus Life

Cafeteria/Auditorium

Students come here everyday.

This is where students of all grades and ages come and eat. They can either buy lunch or bring it from home. This is also where students are called for school assemblies. Assemblies can start in the morning or they can start later in the day. Assemblies are called for various reasons such as awards or showcases.



Playground/Basketball Court

Students get a break from working and come outside to play.

The playground has two parts. They are both created similarly since they both have slides and stairs to get to the top level. There are also monkey bars and swings as well. Around the playground, there is a gravel track which kids run on. There is a field inside of the track for soccer or american football to take place. The field is very spacious and green as well. There is tetherball along the track as well. Aside from all those, there is a giant cement court used for basketball, hopscotch and chalk drawings.

Achievements

Sommer Character Award
Received in 3rd grade.

The award is given to students for their efforts in demonstrating exemplary character and being the "Key To Our Future" at Patsy Sommer Elementary.

Perfect Attendance
Received in 3rd grade.

A perfect attendance award is traditionally given at the end of the school year in U.S. schools to honor students who were present for every day of school during that year, or in some cases across multiple years. Supporters believe that the award promotes education by encouraging students to attend class.

Honor Roll
Received in 3rd grade.

The purpose of an Honor Roll is to provide student recognition for academic achievement. Honor roll recognition will be given at the end of each marking period for students in grades six through twelve.
